Kasi Avocado and Rotti dish.

This is our Kasi Avocado and Rotti dish enjoy it with pack of chips, for a filling meal.

Ingredients of our Avocado and Rotti dish.

  • Fresh ripe avocado
  • Natural rock salt
  • Flour
  • Spring water

Preparation steps of our dish.

  1. Mix your flour and water to make a dough.
  2. When your dough is ready, prepare a pan with hot oil.
  3. Roll the dough into thin slices of circles.
  5. Fry each side of the Rotti for about 3 min at high heat.
  6. Cut avocado into two halves, and mix it in bowl.
  7. Add a pinch of salt, and enjoy.
