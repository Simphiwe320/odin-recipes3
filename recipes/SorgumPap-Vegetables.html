Kasi Sorgum Pap and Vegetables dish.

This is our Kasi Sorgum Pap and stir fried vegetables, enjoy it with some steak for a filling meal.

Ingredients of our Sorgum Pap and Stir fried Vegetables dish.

  • Mabele grains
  • Water
  • Natural rock salt
  • A mix of vegetables

Preparation steps of our dish.

  1. Mix your grains with boiling water in a stove, at high temp.
  2. Cooking till all the water evaporates, and becomes pap like.
  3. Cook it for about 15 - 20 min, then it is ready.
  4. Pour all your veges in a pan with a hot coconut oil.
  5. Stir fry them till you reach your desired preference.
  6. Add your prefered spices, for about 15 - 20 min and enjoy.
